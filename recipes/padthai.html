Pad Thai

Description

Pad Thai is legendary street food. You can find it on nearly every street corner in Thailand.

A popular Thai dish that combines stir-fried rice noodles with eggs, tofu, and chicken or another protein. Once you toss all of this delicious food with a sour, sweet, salty sauce, you are guaranteed to enjoy it. And pad thai is generally served with toppings like peanuts, fresh vegetables, bean sprouts, and cilantro.

Ingredients

  • Rice noodles
  • Shrimp
  • Eggs
  • Firm tofu
  • Preserved radish
  • Bean sprouts
  • Chinese chives
  • Garlic
  • Shallots
  • Dried shrimp
  • Tamarind paste
  • Palm sugar
  • Fish saurce

Steps

  1. Soak noodles in room-temperature water until flexible, then drain.
  2. Mix sauce by heating tamarind paste, palm sugar, and fish sauce until dissolved and balanced.
  3. Stir-fry aromatics (oil, garlic, shallots, dried shrimp) in a hot wok until fragrant.
  4. Add protein and tofu, cook just until done.
  5. Push aside, scramble eggs, then mix everything together.
  6. Add noodles and sauce, toss quickly until noodles absorb sauce and lightly caramelize.
  7. Stir in preserved radish, then finish with bean sprouts and chives.
  8. Serve immediately with peanuts, lime, and chili flakes.
