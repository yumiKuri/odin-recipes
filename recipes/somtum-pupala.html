Pad Thai

Description

a bold expression of northeastern Thai cuisine, built on crisp green papaya and seasoned with lime, chilies, and the unmistakable depth of fermented fish sauce. Its aroma is intense, its flavors unapologetically raw, balanced by fresh vegetables that bring brightness and texture.

On the palate, sourness, heat, and deep umami unfold in quick succession, creating a dish that is both challenging and deeply satisfying. Rooted in tradition, somtam pla ra offers an authentic taste of Thailand — vibrant, expressive, and unforgettable.

Ingredients

  • Green papaya
  • Pla ra (fermeted fish saurce)
  • Chilies
  • Garlic
  • Lime
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Long beans
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Pound chilies and garlic lightly in a mortar until fragrant.
  2. Add long beans and tomatoes; gently bruise to release juices.
  3. Season with pla ra and lime juice (adjust to taste).
  4. Mix in shredded green papaya and lightly pound/toss until coated.
  5. Taste & adjust—spicy, sour, salty should dominate. Serve fresh.
